OurPart

Becoming a programmer is hard work. We will ensure you focus on the right challenges and support you in facing them. After the first six months, we will help you find the right practicum - an Israeli or German startup, a NGO, or an open source project - that will help you finish this year of training not only with a fancy portfolio but with a real-world coding experience that will prepare you best for your first tech job. We will be there with you through the practicum period to make sure you take all that can be taken from this great experience, and to help you succeed in what we see as a general rehearsal before going to market.

AboutTheCourse

In the course, we will learn the most needed technologies in the industry: the essentials JavaScript, HTML, CSS, the framework React for front-hand, node.JS and Express for back-hand, and the non relational database MongoDB. On the practicum, you will possibly learn more technologies according to the project requirements and needs.
